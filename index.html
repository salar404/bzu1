Sub-campuses

Layyah Campus / Southern Punjab
Layyah Campus of Bahauddin Zakariya University in Pakistan aims to provide quality education and academic facilities to students in the Layyah region.
Vehari Campus /Southern Punjab
Vehari Campus serves as an educational outpost, contributing to the academic growth of students in the Vehari region, with a commitment to quality education and research.
Sahiwal Campus/ Central Punjab
Sahiwal Campus extends BZU's academic reach, fostering education and research excellence in the Sahiwal region.The campus is dedicated to providing accessible higher education
